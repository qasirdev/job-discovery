# ADTECH DOMAIN CONTEXT

The job discovery platform utilizes concepts and integrations specific to the AdTech ecosystem to intelligently match candidates with roles in that industry.

## Ecosystem Knowledge
The platform requires contextual awareness of:
- **SSP/DSP Ecosystems**: Supply-Side Platforms and Demand-Side Platforms.
- **Key Industry Players**: Google Ad Manager, StackAdapt, The Trade Desk.

## Technical Context
- **Bid Request Timing**: Awareness of extreme low-latency environments (~50–120ms) required for adtech roles.

## Application
This context is injected into the AI Prompts for:
- **Recruiter Intelligence**: Better matching candidate skills to highly technical adtech recruiter requirements.
- **AdTech Job Scoring**: Rewarding roles that align with programmatic advertising skill sets.
