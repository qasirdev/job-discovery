<tone_rules>
- Use a professional yet modern tone. Avoid overly formal or archaic language (e.g., "Enclosed please find", "To whom it may concern").
- Be confident but not arrogant.
- Show enthusiasm for the company's mission and the specific role.
- For startups, use a slightly more conversational and passionate tone.
- For enterprise/finance, use a highly structured, data-driven tone.
</tone_rules>
